Take a look at some of our best work.

Project 1

Fundraise

We created a charity, Hop Across America, to help rescue and find fur-ever homes for bunnies. Over the past couple of years, we have raised $5,000 towards food supplies, toys, and medical bills for rabbit shelters. Donations poured in from all over the US and we hope to soon make a presence internationally. So far we have sponsored Warwick Valley Humane Society, Bunnies and Beyond, and the Long Island Rabbit Rescue Group.

Project 2

Educate

Our team visited local schools to educate younger students about rabbit care as well as other small animals. During these visits we brought bunnies to demonstrate how to handle them properly without causing harm or stress.

Project 3

Connect

We created a weekly newspaper to update our followers of current events within the rabbit community and generate interest in potentially fostering/adopting a rabbit because shelters are constantly over run or lack the proper space to house rabbit rescues. We hope to create a community where people can learn about proper rabbit care and deepen the connections between local organizations to save more rabbits in need.
